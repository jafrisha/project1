Personal Background

Both my parents were born in Pakistan, and they immigrated to the United States in the early 1980s, shortly before I was born on August 22, 1984. I was born and originally lived in New York City, New York, but have resided in the quiet suburbs of central New Jersey for most of my life. From a young age, I was fasinated by computers and technology, and spent countless hours enjoying my first desktop PC in 1994, a Compaq Presario model running the Windows 3.1x operating system. During those formative years, my life-long interest in computers began taking root. In later years, I frequently had the opportunity to share my knowledge or skills with others when troubleshooting the inevitable problems that can arise with computer hardware and/or software. When I began planning my college education, it was extremely clear that a career in Information Technology would be a perfect fit for me.
